Ariana Grande

Ariana Grande, Grammy-winning singer and actor started her career in her teenage years, landing a role in the Broadway musical 13. After that, she made her big break as Cat Valentine on the Nickelodeon show Victorious and its spinoff, Sam & Cat.

Her debut album, Yours Truly, came out in 2013, giving way to her chart-topping music career. She released seven albums in nine years, earning two Grammy Awards in the process. Grande then returned to acting and musicals, starring Glinda in the 2024 movie adaptation of Wicked. She was nominated for Best Supporting Actress in Any Motion Picture at the 2025 Golden Globes.

As a cultural icon, Swift influences fashion, feminism, and fan culture. She advocates for artists' rights and LGBTQ+ causes, using her platform to promote positivity. In today's generation, she represents resilience and authenticity in the music industry.
